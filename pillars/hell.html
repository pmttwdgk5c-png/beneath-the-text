On method

Every claim rests on a primary source you can open yourself. Don't trust the writer. Trust the documents — and your own reading of them.

The standpoint is one of faith, not against it: a conviction that honest questions are owed honest sources, and that a two-thousand-year, worldwide faith can bear them.
